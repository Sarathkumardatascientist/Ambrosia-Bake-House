About Us

Ambrosia Bake House, with over 24 years of baking heritage, is a beloved name known for its fresh, wholesome, and flavorful creations. Operating from a dedicated central kitchen, we ensure top-notch quality across all our offerings—from artisanal breads and savory snacks to exquisite cakes and pastries. With 3 full-service outlets and 2 convenient pickup counters, we are deeply rooted in the community, committed to using only the freshest ingredients,and driven by a passion for food that brings joy. At Ambrosia, baking isn’t just a craft—it’s a tradition of love, care,and customer-first service.
